To verify the Strimzi operator:
  kubectl rollout status deployment/{{ .Values.operator.fullnameOverride }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To check Kafka readiness:
  kubectl rollout status statefulset/{{ .Values.kafkaCluster.name }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To port-forward to Kafka:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ .Values.kafkaCluster.name }}-bootstrap 9092:9092 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
